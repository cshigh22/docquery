# Trip Notes: North Carolina (Triangle Area)
Dates: 4/24 - 4/26

## 4/24: Arrival & Chapel Hill (UNC)
*   Landed at RDU airport. Picked up the rental car. ($120 for the weekend)
*   Drove straight to Chapel Hill. Walked around the UNC campus. The quad is beautiful in the spring. 
*   Lunch: Grabbed a chicken cheddar biscuit on Franklin Street. ($9.50)
*   Bought a UNC Tar Heels hoodie at the student store. ($55.00)
*   Dinner: Brewery on Franklin. Great view of the downtown area from the rooftop. ($42.00)
*   Hotel check-in near the campus. ($150/night)

## 4/25: Durham (Duke University)
*   Morning coffee at a local cafe. ($5.50)
*   Drove over to Durham to check out Duke. The Gothic architecture is insane – literally looks like Hogwarts.
*   Walked through the Duke Chapel and the Sarah P. Duke Gardens. Entry was free, but campus parking was a hassle. ($10 parking)
*   Lunch: Pub near the American Tobacco Campus. ($18.00)
*   Dinner: Tapas bar in downtown Durham. Incredible food, heavily recommend. ($75.00)
*   Uber back to the hotel since we had a few drinks. ($22.00)

## 4/26: Wrap Up & Departure
*   Quick breakfast at the hotel. (Free)
*   Last-minute stop at a local bakery for airport snacks. ($12.00)
*   Topped off gas for the rental car before dropping it off. ($28.00)
*   Flight out of RDU at 1:00 PM. 

**Total Tracked Expenses:** ~$547.00
