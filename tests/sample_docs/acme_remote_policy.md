# Acme Robotics Remote Work Policy

Acme Robotics supports a hybrid work model. Engineers may work remotely up to three days per week, provided they attend in-person standups every Tuesday and Thursday at the San Jose headquarters.

All remote work must be performed from a location with a stable internet connection of at least 50 Mbps. Employees working outside California for more than 30 consecutive days must notify People Ops in advance for tax purposes.

## Equipment & Stipend

Equipment requests should be submitted through the internal IT portal. The standard remote-work stipend is $75 per month, intended to offset internet and utility costs. Reimbursement for ergonomic furniture is capped at $400 per calendar year.
